The 5 warning signs

Read each one. If it feels familiar, that is the one to watch.

01

Slow decisions

How long does it take between a decision and action? If it takes too long, the business loses speed.

02

Mixed roles

Ask three people who owns the work. If you get different answers, the problem is not execution. It is unclear ownership.

03

Too many priorities

How many priorities does each person carry? If there are too many, focus gets lost and work slows down.

04

Same fires repeat

The same problem keeps coming back. That is a sign the root cause has not been fixed.

05

No clear owner

When pressure hits, who decides? If the answer changes, people lose time waiting for direction.

One or more of these feel familiar?

That is not a strategy gap. That is a clarity problem.
