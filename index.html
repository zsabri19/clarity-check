Five signs

Why Work Slows

Your team may be busy, but still unclear. These five signs show what may be happening underneath.

Read them first. Then check your business.
Five signs

Read the five signs. See which one feels familiar.

If more than one feels true, that is normal. The test will help show which problem is putting the most pressure on the business.

01

Decision drag

Decisions are made, but action still takes too long. People agree, then the work slows down.

02

Ownership drift

When pressure rises, ownership starts moving around. People help, but nobody clearly owns the final result.

03

Priority overload

Everyone is carrying too much at once. The team stays busy, but the important work still does not finish cleanly.

04

Repeat fires

The same problems keep coming back. People fix them again and again, but the business does not learn from the fix.

05

Hero dependency

Progress depends on one person stepping in. The business works, but it leans too much on personal rescue.
